FAQ

The page or prediction got stuck on initializing.

Please delete your browser cache. Restart your browser and try again. If it still does not work, please try to use another browser.

How to check whether any news is real or fake ?

Enter the required news info and click on Check button, the Machine learning model will validate the prediction.

Where can we see the news validation output of real or fake ?

You can see it just below the prediction button.

I am getting an error message.

This can be due to mutiple reasons. Please be sure: • you have entered the title of the news • you have entered the text of the news • you have entered the author/source of the news correctly.

Are title,text and author are compulsory fields ?

Yes, Title,text and author are compulsory fields.
